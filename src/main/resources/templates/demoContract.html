Materials and Expenses

Supplier shall be responsible for all materials and expenses incurred while performing services under this Agreement.

Independent Supplier Status

Supplier is an independent Supplier, not Aggregator's employee. Supplier's employees or contract personnel are not Aggregator's employees.

Business Permits, Certificates, and Licenses

Supplier has complied with all federal, state, and local laws requiring business permits, certificates, and licenses required to carry out the services to be performed under this Agreement. Supplier will provide Aggregator with a copy of all relevant permits, certificates and licenses.

Criminal Background Check

Supplier must undergo a complete criminal background check including fingerprinting and provide a copy of the results to Aggregator. Supplier is responsible for all expenses related to the criminal background check. Supplier has the duty to update and disclose to Aggregator any change in criminal record after the background check has been submitted.

State and Federal Taxes

Aggregator will not: withhold FICA (Social Security and Medicare taxes) from Supplier's payments or make FICA payments on Supplier's behalf, make state or federal unemployment compensation contributions on Supplier's behalf, or withhold state or federal income tax from Supplier's payments.

Supplier shall pay all taxes incurred while performing services under this Agreement--including all applicable income taxes and, if Supplier is not a corporation, self-employment (Social Security) taxes. Upon demand, Supplier shall provide Aggregator with proof that such payments have been made.

Fringe Benefits

Supplier understands that neither Supplier nor Supplier's employees or contract personnel are eligible to participate in any employee pension, profit sharing, health, vacation pay, sick pay, or other fringe benefit plan of Aggregator. If Supplier is later classified as Aggregator's employee, Supplier expressly waives Supplier's rights to any benefits to which he or she was, or might have become, entitled.

Workers' Compensation

Aggregator shall not obtain workers' compensation insurance on behalf of Supplier or Supplier's employees. If Supplier hires employees to perform any work under this Agreement, Supplier will cover them with workers' compensation insurance and provide Aggregator with a certificate of workers' compensation insurance before the employees begin the work.

Unemployment Compensation

Aggregator shall make no state or federal unemployment compensation payments on behalf of Supplier or Supplier's employees or contract personnel. Supplier will not be entitled to these benefits in connection with work performed under this Agreement.

Insurance

Aggregator shall not provide any insurance coverage of any kind for Supplier or Supplier's employees or contract personnel. Additional insurance requirements may be required and will be addressed on a case by case basis.

Supplier shall indemnify and hold Aggregator harmless from any loss or liability arising from performing services under this Agreement.

Non-Disclosure of Customer Lists and other Proprietary Information

Supplier agrees not to disclose proprietary information about Aggregator, its operations, clientele or other information relating to the organization. This includes, but not limited to, the names and contact information of its customers, marketing strategies, operations and any other confidential information.

Supplier shall not, during the contract period and for a period of one year immediately following termination of the agreement, without written permission from Aggregator, either directly or indirectly, call on, solicit, or take away any customers of Aggregator on whom Supplier called or became acquainted with during the terms of the agreement, either for their own benefit or for the benefit of any other person, firm or organization.

Non-Recruit Covenant

Supplier shall not, during this agreement and for a period of one year following the termination of this agreement, either directly or indirectly, recruit any of Aggregator's employees for the purpose of outside business.

Legal Compliance

Supplier is encouraged to treat all Aggregator employees, customers, clients, business partners and other affiliates with respect and responsibility. Supplier is required to comply with all laws, ethical codes, and Aggregator policies, procedures or rules including those forbidding harassment, discrimination and unfair business practices.

Indemnification

Supplier shall defend, indemnify, hold harmless and insure Aggregator from any and all damages, expenses or liability resulting from or arising out of any negligence or misconduct on Supplier's part, or from any breach or default of this agreement which is caused by the acts of Supplier. Supplier shall insure that its employees and affiliates take all actions necessary to comply the the terms set forth in this agreement. Supplier shall name Aggregator as additional insured on all related insurance policies including general liability.

Terminating the Agreement

Either party may terminate this Agreement any time by giving written notice to the other party of the intent to terminate.

Exclusive Agreement

Supplier will not use services in Washington DC from other suppliers for up to [[${exclusivityPeriod}]] years starting from [[${dateStart}]].

Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is determined to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain in full force if the essential provisions of this Agreement for Aggregator and Supplier remain valid, binding, and enforceable.

Applicable Law

This Agreement will be governed by the laws of the state of Delaware.

No Partnership

This Agreement does not create a partnership relationship. Supplier does not have authority to enter into contracts on Aggregator's behalf.

Resolving Disputes

If a dispute arises under this Agreement, any party may take the matter to court.
